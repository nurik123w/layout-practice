Hasbik refused to fight with Abdurosik

Khasbik is not ready to enter the ring against Abdurosik – the blogger was not satisfied with the conditions offered by the organizer of the fight Askhab Tamayev. Khasbulla Magomedov was offered 5-7 million rubles, which is much less than the amount that the fighter requires. As Tamayev notes, he successfully discussed all the conditions with the second party.

The duel between the blogger Khaybula Magomedov, known by the nickname Khasbik, and the singer Abdurosik will not take place. As it became known from a new video on the YouTube channel of the organizer Askhab Tamayev, there will be no fight due to the increased demands of Hasbik.

"Initially, we agreed with Khasbulla for the amount of 1 million rubles. Then it was a lot of money for Hasbullah. I gave him 20 thousand rubles for the video, but recently-500 thousand rubles, " Tamayev said and noted that he was ready to offer Hasbik 5 million rubles for the fight. The organizer noted that it was possible to agree with Abdurosik, but he will not voice the singer's fee.At the end of the video, the organizer still met with Hasbik and was refused – Hasbull will not fight for 5 or even 7 million. "This is very little for such a fight. How much do I need? I said a long time ago-20 million rubles. For the video-1 million or 50-50 pay, " Hasbik snapped.
